ABOUT ME

A Dublin-born writer now based in New York, he is a masterful wordsmith with a profound appreciation for history and storytelling. Having studied History at Trinity College Dublin, he furthered his passion by obtaining a Master's in creative writing and is currently completing a PhD in Folk Music History. Jack's captivating prose has been published in prestigious global publications, where he skillfully intertwines themes of humanity, culture, and the longing for connection. Drawing inspiration from the vibrant energy of New York City and his Irish roots, Jack's writing effortlessly transports readers on introspective journeys of exploration and self-discovery.
