ABOUT JACK

Jack Sheehan is a writer, historian, editor, and photographer from Dublin, Ireland, based in New York. He holds an M.Phil. in Creative Writing and a PhD in Modern Irish and U.S. History from Trinity College Dublin. His work has appeared in The Baffler, The Washington Post, The Irish Times, The New York Times, and The London Magazine, among others. He lives in Brooklyn with his wife. https://jacksheehan.substack.com/ Contact/Commissions: jackosiochain@gmail.com
