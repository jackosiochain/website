ABOUT JACK

Jack Sheehan is a writer, historian, editor and photographer from Dublin, Ireland, based in New York. He graduated with an MPhil in Creative Writing from Trinity College Dublin in 2016, and recently completed a PhD in Modern Irish and U.S. History from the same institution. He was named a Young Writer Delegate at the 2018 Cúirt International Festival of Literature. His fiction, essays and reviews have appeared in The New York Times, The Baffler, The Washington Post, The Irish Times, Lonely Planet, The Fence, LitHub, Tribune and The London Magazine, among others. He lives in Harlem with his wife. Contact/Commissions: jackosiochain@gmail.com
